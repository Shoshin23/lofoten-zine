Wed 19 · Land & roll in🛏 Svolvær · Storgata 18
🚗
Evenes → Svolvær — Google 2h39 (166 km) · plan ~3h15. Add ~30 min for the Austnesfjord & Sildpollnes stops. Car pickup: allow 30–45 min first.
  • Land AMS → Evenes (EVE) 16:40. Pick up the VW ID.3 (electric); big shop best done in Svolvær on arrival (no good supermarket on the way).
  • Drive the E10 into Lofoten — gorgeous even as a transfer, hours of evening light left.
  • On-route stops near Svolvær: Austnesfjorden viewpoint 2 min & Sildpollnes Church on its fjord spit. photo stop
  • Check in at Storgata 18, Svolvær, cook the first dinner. 🍝
⚡ Charge the ID.3 overnight or at the big Svolvær fast-charger bank (Recharge/Mer/Circle K) — contactless or the Elton app. Full charging plan ↓
Thu 20 · Henningsvær & the beaches🛏 Move west → Sørvågen (Tind)
🚗
The driving-heavy day ≈ 4h35 total. Svolvær→Henningsvær ~50m · →Leknes ~1h30 · →Haukland ~30m · →Skagsanden ~1h · →Reine ~55m. (Google base ~3h10 + buffer.) Start early.
  • Henningsvær — harbour, galleries, clifftop football pitch. easy Optional Festvågtind. medium ~2h
  • Haukland ↔ Uttakleiv coastal walk around Mannen — two white-sand beaches, one gentle path. easy ~1–2h
  • Optional scramble up Mannen for a top-down view of both beaches. short & steep optional
  • Stop at Skagsanden beach, then settle into your Tind cabin (Sørvågen). Cook in.
🛒 Groceries in Leknes (en route). ⚡ Charge here too — Leknes is the mid-island fast-charge hub, ideal mid-drive. If the day runs long, drop Skagsanden.
Fri 21 · The deep west🛏 Sørvågen (Tind) · Tindsveien 5
🚗
Barely any driving — your Tind base sits in the middle of it all. From Tind: Å/Ågvatnet ~4 min, Sørvågen waterfall ~5 min, Reine/Hamnøy/Reinebringen ~12 min. Everything else on foot.
  • Reine, Hamnøy & Sakrisøy — the most photographed corner of Norway. Roadside viewpoints, zero climbing. easy
  • Å i Lofoten + the Ågvatnet lake loop — a calm fjord-lake ringed by peaks. easy
  • Sørvågen Waterfall (Studalselva) — a short ~1 km stroll to a cascade with a fjord view. 💧 waterfall easy ~30 min
  • Reinebringen stone stairs for the postcard shot. medium-hard ~1.5h easy swap: bridge viewpoints
💧 More water & alpine drama: the Sørvågen → Munkebu trail climbs past waterfalls, streams & mountain lakes — go as far as you like.
Sat 22 · Kvalvika + Ryten, then east🛏 Back to Svolvær · Storgata 18
🚗
The long day. Tind→Fredvang ~45m, hike, then Fredvang→Svolvær ~2h35 (Google 1h51, 105 km). ⚡ Charge at Leknes on the way east. Hike early, eat, then drive.
  • Kvalvika Beach + Ryten — a hidden beach and a clifftop view straight down onto it. medium ~3–4h easier: just Kvalvika beach
  • 📸 Fredvang roadside stop: the shark-fin peak Volandstinden + the curving Fredvang Bridges with red cabins — the classic shot, right where you park. no hike
  • Swap option: want that view from above? Volandstinden is a separate short hike (~5 km, easy-moderate) from Fredvang — trade it for Ryten if the shark-fin-from-above is the dream. pick one
  • Drive back east to Svolvær. Optional easy viewpoint near Kabelvåg: Tjeldbergtind. easy-medium optional
⛰️ Ryten looks out over Kvalvika & the open sea — a different angle from the Volandstinden/bridges view. You realistically can’t do both big hikes plus the drive east, so choose your peak.
Sun 23 · Slow morning, fly home🛫 Drive to Evenes (EVE)
🚗
Svolvær → Evenes ~3h05 (Google 2h39, 166 km). Leave by ~09:00–09:15 for the 13:40 flight with car return + check-in.
  • Relaxed start: Svolvær harbour, a bakery stop, pack up.
  • Charge to full in Svolvær before leaving — 166 km is well within the ID.3's range. Top-up chargers near Narvik/Bjerkvik if you want one before drop-off.
  • Fly EVE → Amsterdam. 🎉
⏱️ Staying in Svolvær (not Reine) the last night is the whole trick — Reine → Evenes is ~4 h, Svolvær ~2.5 h. That’s why Saturday loops back east.

Power · the ID.3

Charging the car

tap ⚡ on the map ↑

The VW ID.3 (~58 kWh, ~300 km real range) fast-charges ~10→80% in ~30 min. Norway is the easiest place on earth to charge — you’ll see Recharge, Mer, Circle K & Eviny. Pay by contactless card (works at every fast charger in 2026, no account needed).

📲 Elton — one app, most networks 🗺️ ABRP — plan the day ⚡ PlugShare / ladestasjoner.no — live status 💳 contactless — simplest backup
  • ⚡
    Wed · Svolvær — charge to full overnight or at the big Svolvær bank after the drive in. Start every day topped up.
  • ⚡
    Thu · Leknes — the mid-island fast-charge hub. Plug in while you grocery-shop, then push west to Tind. Recommended pit stop.
  • ○
    Fri · Tind — barely any driving today, so no fast charge needed. Reine has slower AC poles; trickle-charge at the cabin if it has a socket.
  • ⚡
    Sat · Leknes (eastbound) — top up on the long drive back to Svolvær (Ramberg / Lofoten Beach Camp is a backup). Recommended pit stop.
  • ⚡
    Sun · Svolvær → Evenes — leave Svolvær full; 166 km is well within range. Chargers near Narvik/Bjerkvik for a final top-up before drop-off.

Practical

Good to know

Driving + EV

  1. One main road (E10), well-kept, calm drivers — easy if you’ve driven in Europe.
  2. West end (Reine/Flakstad) has narrow bits & single-lane bridges — slow, let oncoming cars pass.
  3. Never stop in the road for photos. Use marked laybys only.
  4. Summer traffic is slower than Google shows — that’s why every leg here has a buffer.
  5. Charging: the ID.3 does ~10→80% in ~30 min. Fast-charge hubs in Svolvær, Leknes & Evenes; pay by contactless or the Elton app (2026). See the Charging section.
