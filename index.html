Mon 17 · Touch down & roll in🛏 Base: Svolvær / Kabelvåg
  • Fly Amsterdam → Evenes (EVE), the Harstad–Narvik airport. Aim for a midday-ish arrival.
  • Pick up the rental car. Big grocery shop at the first Rema 1000 / Coop Extra — stock up for veg cooking while choice is good.
  • Drive into Lofoten on the E10 (~2.5–3 h). It's jaw-dropping even as a transfer — fjords, bridges, peaks.
  • Check in near Svolvær / Kabelvåg. Easy evening: harbour stroll, cook the first dinner. chill
✈️ Book the AMS↔EVE flight first — it's the single biggest cost lever. Target €300–350 return.
Tue 18 · Henningsvær & east lakes🛏 Base: Svolvær / Kabelvåg
  • Henningsvær — the "Venice of Lofoten," a village scattered over skerries. Wander the harbour + the famous clifftop football pitch. easy
  • Optional climb: Festvågtind for a top-down view of the village. medium ~2h optional
  • Roadside lakes & fjord viewpoints (Storvatnet area) on the way back — pull into laybys for photos.
  • Cook-in dinner, or treat yourselves to a veg pizza in Svolvær.
📸 Golden hour runs late — around 9–10pm in August. Save a viewpoint for the soft evening light.
Wed 19 · White-sand beach day🛏 Move west → Reine / Hamnøy
  • Drive west on the E10 (1.5–2 h with stops).
  • Haukland → Uttakleiv coastal path — turquoise water, white sand, the best short walk in Lofoten. easy ~1–2h
  • Stop at Skagsanden beach & Flakstad church en route; maybe Vikten glass studio.
  • Check into Reine / Hamnøy / Ramberg base — your home for the dramatic west end. Cook in.
🛒 Shops thin out further west — do a top-up grocery run in Leknes before settling in Reine.
Thu 20 · Reine, Å & the big view🛏 Base: Reine / Hamnøy
  • Reine & Hamnøy — the most photographed spot in Norway. Red rorbuer under razor peaks; viewpoints from the bridges & Sakrisøy. easy
  • Å i Lofoten — village at the literal end of the road. Walk the Ågvatnet lake loop, a calm fjord-lake ringed by mountains. easy
  • Pick ONE big nature hit:
    • Kvalvika Beach + Ryten viewpoint over a hidden beach medium ~3–4h
    • or Reinebringen stair-climb for the postcard shot medium-hard
    • or keep it gentle — viewpoints only easy swap
🥾 Doing Ryten or Reinebringen? Go in the morning, carry water + a windproof layer, and wear grippy shoes — trails get slick after rain.
Fri 21 · Slow morning, fly home🛫 Drive to Evenes (EVE)
  • Lazy start: last Reine / Sakrisøy photos, a warm bakery stop, pack up.
  • Drive back east to Evenes (EVE) (~3–3.5 h with the inbound stops you skipped). Leave a comfortable buffer.
  • Drop the car, fly EVE → Amsterdam. 🎉
⛽ Return the car with a full tank (fuel near the airport is easiest) to dodge refuelling fees.

🚗 Good to know

Driving the E10 — easy, just mind these

  1. One main road, well-kept, calm drivers — totally fine if you've driven in Europe.
  2. West end (Reine/Flakstad) has narrow bits & single-lane bridges — slow, let oncoming cars pass.
  3. Never stop in the road for photos. Use marked laybys only.
  4. Tunnels: conditions can differ at the far end — ease out, don't gun it.
  5. Respect 50–60 km/h zones — villages, sheep & crossings.
