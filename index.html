Wed 19 · Land & roll in🛏 Base 1: Svolvær / Kabelvåg
  • Land Amsterdam → Evenes (EVE) at 16:40. Pick up the electric car; big shop at the first Rema 1000 / Coop Extra.
  • Drive the E10 into Lofoten (~2.5 h) — gorgeous even as a transfer, and you'll have hours of evening light.
  • Evening stops near Svolvær: Austnesfjorden viewpoint (boardwalk over the fjord) 2 min and Sildpollnes Church — the white chapel alone on a fjord spit, magic in low light. photo stop
  • Check in around Svolvær / Kabelvåg, cook the first dinner. 🍝
⚡ Top up the EV at the big Svolvær fast-charger bank when convenient — sets you up for the next few days.
Thu 20 · Henningsvær & the beaches🛏 Move west → Reine / Hamnøy
  • Henningsvær — the "Venice of Lofoten." Harbour, galleries, the clifftop football pitch. easy
  • Optional climb: Festvågtind over the village. medium ~2h optional
  • Drive west; do the Haukland ↔ Uttakleiv coastal walk around Mannen — two white-sand beaches linked by a gentle path. easy ~1–2h
  • Quick add: scramble up Mannen for a top-down view of both beaches. short & steep optional
  • Stop at Skagsanden beach, then settle into Reine / Hamnøy. Cook in.
🛒 Shops thin out west — top up groceries in Leknes before Reine. ⚡ Fast chargers in Leknes too.
Fri 21 · The deep west🛏 Base 2: Reine / Hamnøy
  • Reine, Hamnøy & Sakrisøy — the most photographed corner of Norway. Roadside viewpoints galore, zero climbing. easy
  • Å i Lofoten + the Ågvatnet lake loop — a calm fjord-lake ringed by peaks. easy
  • Sørvågen Waterfall (Studalselva) — a short, well-marked ~1 km stroll from the car to a cascade with a fjord view. 💧 waterfall easy ~30 min
  • Reinebringen stone stairs for the postcard shot. medium-hard ~1.5h — or skip and enjoy the bridge viewpoints. easy swap
💧 Want more water & alpine drama? The Sørvågen → Munkebu trail climbs past waterfalls, streams and mountain lakes — go as far as you like, then turn back.
Sat 22 · Kvalvika + Ryten, then east🛏 Back to Svolvær
  • Morning hike: Kvalvika Beach + Ryten — a hidden beach and a clifftop view straight down onto it. One of Lofoten's best, doable at an easy-medium pace. medium ~3–4h
  • Easier swap: just walk down to Kvalvika beach and skip the Ryten summit. gentler
  • Afternoon: drive back east to Svolvær (~2 h). Last cook-in, or a celebratory veg dinner in town.
  • Optional easy viewpoint near Kabelvåg if you've legs left: Tjeldbergtind. easy-medium ~1.5h optional
🥾 Carry water + a windproof layer for Ryten, wear grippy shoes (slick after rain), and go in the morning to beat the day-trippers.
Sun 23 · Slow morning, fly home🛫 Drive to Evenes (EVE)
  • Relaxed start: Svolvær harbour, a bakery stop, pack up.
  • Drive Svolvær → Evenes (~2.5 h). Leave by ~09:30 to comfortably make the 13:40 flight with car return + check-in.
  • Return the EV fully charged (charger near the airport is easiest), fly EVE → Amsterdam. 🎉
⏱️ Staying in Svolvær (not Reine) on the last night is the whole trick — Reine → Evenes is ~4 h, Svolvær is ~2.5 h. That's why we loop back east on Saturday.

🚗 Good to know

Driving + EV

  1. One main road (E10), well-kept, calm drivers — easy if you've driven in Europe.
  2. West end (Reine/Flakstad) has narrow bits & single-lane bridges — slow, let oncoming cars pass.
  3. Never stop in the road for photos. Use marked laybys only.
  4. Charging is easy: fast-charger banks in Svolvær & Leknes; ~30 min = 10→80%. Cards now work directly (2026).
  5. Last night in Svolvær keeps Sunday's airport drive to ~2.5 h.
